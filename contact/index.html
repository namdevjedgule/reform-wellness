Quick Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the first consultation really free? +
Yes! Your first consultation is completely free and has no obligation. We'll understand your goals, assess your current health, and explain how we can help — before you commit to anything.
Do you offer online / remote consultations? +
Absolutely. All our consultations and follow-ups are available online via WhatsApp, Zoom, or phone call. We serve clients from across India with the same quality of care as in-person visits.
How quickly will I see results? +
Most clients begin noticing meaningful improvements in energy, digestion, and overall wellbeing within 2–3 weeks. Visible physical changes typically appear within 4–8 weeks, depending on your goals and consistency.
Will I have to give up Indian food? +
Never. Our plans are built entirely around Indian cuisine — dal, sabzi, rotis, rice, curd, and everything you love. We believe food should nourish both your body and your culture.
How much do your programs cost? +
Our program pricing varies based on the duration, condition complexity, and level of support required. We offer transparent packages with no hidden costs. Your free consultation will include full pricing details.
What if I have a medical condition like PCOS or diabetes? +
We specialize in therapeutic nutrition for medical conditions including PCOS, thyroid disorders, type 2 diabetes, hypertension, and more. All plans are evidence-based and developed in coordination with your healthcare provider.
